Frequently Asked Questions About Asphalt Milling and Overlay

How is asphalt milling and overlay performed, and how do I know if it's right for my property in Clifton?
Asphalt milling involves removing the top layer of your existing asphalt surface, which is then recycled, before applying a new overlay of fresh asphalt. This process is ideal for properties in Clifton where the base is still structurally sound but the surface is worn or uneven. We assess the current condition of your driveway or parking lot to determine if milling and overlay is a cost-effective solution, or if full replacement is needed.
What factors affect the pricing for asphalt milling and overlay in the Clifton, NJ area?
Pricing depends on several factors, including the total square footage, the extent of milling needed, the thickness of the new asphalt layer, and accessibility for our equipment. In Clifton, we also consider local permit requirements and disposal fees. We provide free, detailed estimates after an on-site evaluation to ensure transparency and accuracy.
How long does the milling and overlay process take, and how soon can I use my driveway or parking lot afterwards?
For most residential driveways or small commercial lots in Clifton, the milling and overlay process can be completed in 1-2 days, depending on weather and project size. After paving, we recommend waiting at least 24 hours before light use, and up to 72 hours before heavy vehicle traffic to ensure optimal curing.
