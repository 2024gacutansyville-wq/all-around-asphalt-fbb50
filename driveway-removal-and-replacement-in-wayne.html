How much does driveway removal and replacement typically cost in Wayne, NJ?
The cost for driveway removal and replacement in Wayne, NJ usually ranges from $6 to $12 per square foot, depending on the size of your driveway, existing conditions, and whether there are any additional requirements like drainage improvements or curb work. At All Around Asphalt, we provide free, no-obligation estimates after a site visit to assess your specific needs and answer any questions about potential costs.
How long does the entire driveway replacement process take, and how soon can I use my new driveway?
For most residential driveways in Wayne, NJ, the removal and replacement process takes about 2-4 days, depending on weather and driveway size. After the new asphalt is laid, we recommend waiting at least 3 days before driving on it, and up to 7 days for heavy vehicles. We’ll give you a detailed timeline before starting and keep you updated throughout the project.

What is your process for removing and replacing a driveway in this area?
Our process starts with a thorough assessment of your existing driveway. We remove the old asphalt and any unstable base material, grade and compact the area, and install a new stone base as needed for proper drainage—important in Wayne due to freeze-thaw cycles. We then lay and compact new hot-mix asphalt, ensuring smooth transitions to sidewalks and garage floors. The site is cleaned up daily, and we coordinate with you to minimize any inconvenience.
